Comprehension Layer Design

What I did

  • Identified key lexical and stylistic features of each literary text
  • Standardized language usage across quizzes, texts, and workbooks
  • Revised instructional and assessment language to maintain consistency across formats

Why it matters

The project operated within a learning context, where users rely on linguistic cues to construct meaning. In this learning environment, inconsistent language use directly affects comprehension, increases cognitive load, and reduces users' willingness to engage with learning materials.

Post-renewal feedback highlighted improved clarity, interaction, and student engagement with literary content.
