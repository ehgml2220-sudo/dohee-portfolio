Human-in-the-loop AI Language Quality

A case page that shows how I define review criteria, align evaluators, and keep output quality stable.

Context

Speech evaluation requires consistent human judgment. I worked on error labels, examples, and decision rules so review stays reliable across large sets.

What I did

  • Defined error taxonomy for segmental and suprasegmental features
  • Wrote criteria and edge-case notes to reduce reviewer drift
  • Shared feedback to refine guidelines and raise consistency

Why it matters for Netflix Product

Member trust depends on language quality. Human review protects nuance and brand voice where automation alone may fail.
